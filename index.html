Season 1 of the HCA is Here
The Hockey Club Association officially launches with 18 franchises across two conferences. All games are played CPU vs CPU in NHL26 and streamed live on Twitch at tml_ttv. Rosters are set — it's time to play hockey.

How the HCA Works
Each franchise is owned and managed by a real GM. Trades, signings, and line changes are submitted to the commissioner group and applied in-game. Every game is streamed live on Twitch for everyone to follow along.
