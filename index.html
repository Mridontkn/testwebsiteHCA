Season 14 of the HCA is Here
The Hockey Club Association officially launches with 18 franchises across two conferences. All games are played CPU vs CPU in NHL26 and streamed on YouTube at @HCANetwork. Rosters are locked in — it's time to play hockey.

How the HCA Works
Each franchise is owned and managed by a real GM. Trades, signings, and line changes are submitted to the commissioner group and applied in-game. Every game is streamed live on YouTube for everyone to follow along.
